Pro Advice

Tips to Create Your Own Secret Drink

The best part of the 7 Brew secret menu is that you can create your own drink. Follow these tips to craft the perfect custom beverage every time.

  • Start by choosing a base — coffee, energy drink, or tea — before adding flavors.
  • Add one or two main flavors and finish with a complementary flavor to balance the taste.
  • Avoid mixing too many flavors at once, as it can overpower the drink. Simple combinations often taste better.
  • Experiment with different milk options like almond or oat milk to change the texture and taste.
  • Ask your barista for a recommendation — they often know the best hidden combinations.
